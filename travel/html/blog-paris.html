Explore Paris

Paris, the capital of France, is known as the "City of Light" and the "City of Love." It offers a unique blend of historic landmarks, world-class museums, elegant architecture, and a rich café culture. Whether you're admiring the Eiffel Tower or strolling along the Seine, Paris is full of romance and charm.

Top Attractions:

  • Eiffel Tower
  • Louvre Museum
  • Notre-Dame Cathedral
  • Montmartre District

Things to Do:

  • Take a sunset cruise on the Seine River
  • Enjoy fresh croissants and coffee at a street café
  • Shop on the Champs-Élysées
  • Visit world-famous art galleries
