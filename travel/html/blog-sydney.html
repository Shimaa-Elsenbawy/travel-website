Explore Sydney

Sydney, Australia's largest city, is famous for its iconic landmarks, beautiful beaches, and vibrant cultural scene along the stunning harbor.

Top Attractions:

  • Sydney Opera House
  • Harbour Bridge
  • Bondi Beach
  • Darling Harbour

Things to Do:

  • Climb the Harbour Bridge
  • Take a ferry to Manly
  • Surf at Bondi Beach
  • Explore The Rocks district
